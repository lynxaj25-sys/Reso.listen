Drake Announces “Iceman” Album

Drake has officially confirmed his upcoming album Iceman, set to release on May 15, 2026. The announcement came through a viral ice sculpture stunt in Toronto.

The album is expected to be his first solo project since 2023 and includes tracks like “What Did I Miss?”, “Which One”, and “Dog House”.

The rollout has already gone viral with fans gathering around the sculpture.
